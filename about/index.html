I’ve worked on roughly forty DTC ecom accounts in the last four years — apparel, beauty, supplements, home goods, electronics. The pattern that brought me to ROAS calculators specifically: most ecom advertisers, even experienced ones, optimize against reported ROAS while their true contribution-margin ROAS is underwater. Reported ROAS looks great because revenue divided by ad spend doesn’t care about cost of goods, returns, payment processing, or shipping. The CFO does. The CFO’s number is the one that matters.

Why this site exists

The calculator on the homepage is the one I built for client onboarding. New clients show up with a target ROAS in their head that doesn’t map to their actual margin structure. The conversation goes faster when there’s a tool that visualizes the gap between “what your dashboard says you’re making” and “what your bank account says you’re making.” This site is that tool plus the curated stack I’d use to actually move the number.

Day-to-day

  • Bid management oversight on Real AI tools (currently Groas across most ecom accounts).
  • Feed quality and Shopping campaign hygiene; building margin-aware ROAS targets per account.
  • Onboarding new ecom clients: building the first 90 days of their paid program with the right tooling.

I don’t take vendor money for ranking placement on this site. Tool order is editorial. Vendors have offered “sponsored review” deals; I’ve declined them all. The site is funded by the agency’s commercial work, not by the tools it covers.
