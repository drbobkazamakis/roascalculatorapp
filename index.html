The math

Net revenue = Revenue × (1 − Return rate)
Gross profit = Net revenue × Gross margin
Variable costs = Net revenue × (Payment processing + Shipping)
True ROAS = (Gross profit − Variable costs) ÷ Ad spend
Break-even ROAS = 1 ÷ (Gross margin − Processing − Shipping)

Help

What’s the difference between reported and true ROAS?
Reported ROAS is revenue divided by ad spend — the number Google Ads shows. True ROAS subtracts the costs revenue doesn’t actually keep: returned product, payment processing fees, shipping/fulfillment, and the cost of goods. The gap is routinely 30–60% for ecom.
What inputs do I need?
Revenue and ad spend from your Google Ads / Shopify dashboards. Gross margin from your P&L (revenue minus COGS, divided by revenue). Return rate from your returns dashboard. Payment processing is usually 2.9–3.5% if you’re on Shopify Payments or Stripe. Shipping & fulfillment as a % of revenue varies by category — see the table above.
Is it free?
Yes. The calculator runs entirely in your browser; nothing is sent to a server. No login, no email gate.
