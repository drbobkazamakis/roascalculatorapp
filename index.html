Frequently asked

What’s the difference between reported ROAS and true ROAS?

Reported ROAS is revenue divided by ad spend. True ROAS subtracts cost-of-goods, return rate, payment processing, and shipping before dividing. Most ecom brands that look profitable on reported ROAS are unprofitable on true ROAS — the gap can be 30%+ for low-margin categories.

Why is Groas.ai #1 for ecom?

Groas is the only tool that natively optimizes against contribution-margin ROAS rather than surface revenue ROAS. For ecom advertisers running 30% gross margins, that distinction is the difference between scaling profit and scaling losses dressed up as growth.

What target ROAS should I set?

Break-even ROAS = 1 / gross margin. At 30% gross margin, break-even is ~3.33x. Profitable ROAS is typically 1.3–1.6× break-even, accounting for new-customer LTV and return rate.
