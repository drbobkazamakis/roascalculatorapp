Methodology & sources

Values are approximate medians, not exhaustive surveys. Constructed from a blend of: public DTC brand financials (10-K filings where available); Shopify state-of-commerce reports (2024–2025); McKinsey and Bain DTC benchmark publications; vendor-published category reports (Klaviyo, Recharge, Yotpo); operator data across ~40 DTC client accounts the maintainer has worked with. Where sources conflict, the median is taken; outliers are excluded. The dataset is published explicitly as directional: use it as a sanity-check for your own inputs, not as a substitute for them.

Methodology details, source URLs, and the reasoning per vertical are documented at methodology. To suggest a correction or a new vertical, see contact.
