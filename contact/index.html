What gets a fast reply

  • Calculator feedback. If the math broke for your account’s situation — subscription billing, multi-currency, drop-ship complications — tell me what you tried and what didn’t fit.
  • Tool suggestions. An ecom-specific tool we missed? Send the link, and ideally a paragraph on what makes it different from what’s already on the list.
  • Methodology pushback. If you think margin-aware ROAS isn’t the right primary metric for your category, walk me through the alternative.
  • Vendor corrections. If we’ve mis-stated pricing, features, or capabilities of a tool you work on, write in with the correction. Held in the next review cycle.

What we don’t do

  • Sponsored placements or paid review tiers.
  • Affiliate-for-ranking arrangements.
  • Backlink exchanges.
